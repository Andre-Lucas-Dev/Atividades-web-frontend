Baking a Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to the required temperature.
  2. Gather and measure all ingredients.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  4. Mix the wet ingredients in another bowl.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  7. Bake in the oven for the specified time.
  8. Let the cake cool before serving or decorating.
